# Stealth

## Sneak Speed

When you are sneaking you get a bonus to speed, I would suggest that you reduce the FOV effects in your client or you will look like a zoomed out space warrior.

## Ghost Armor

This is Armor that generates over time, but will only last for 1 hit, so if you have no armor, you will have the equivalent of Iron, for 1 hit maximum.
