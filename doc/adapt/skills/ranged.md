# Ranged

## Force Shot

This makes your arrows Faster, hit Harder, and have less Drop when they are flying in the air. It should be noted that this affects **ALL PROJECTILES YOU THROW** from crossbows, fireworks, snowballs etc...

## Arrow Piercing

You can Increase the maximum piercing by increasing the level, Original power and effects that the arrow might have are retained through targets dealing full damage to all affected targets&#x20;

## Arrow Lunge

When you shoot an arrow it throws you in the opposite direction you are aiming, great for a tactical retreat, or dodge from another shot!

## Web Snare

The Armor-up Skill allows you to passively gain armor while you move! The more you move, The more armor you get.&#x20;
